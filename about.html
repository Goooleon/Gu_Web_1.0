Shengfeng Gu
// 顾 盛锋
(A.k.a Gü)

Remains to be a Human Designer in the A.I. infused era.

He trusts tools, algorithms, and methodology for better designing, but relies much on his gut feeling and intuition.

He believes that good design should be honest, but makes up stories for his design sometimes.

He strives to create things that have meanings with elegance, but most of the time crafting nonsense for himself.
