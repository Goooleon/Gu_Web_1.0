Miáo

2016

Miáo is a ceramic dining set design based on the ethnic Miao culture after a cultural and art field trip to Guizhou and Yunnan province in the autum of 2016. Inspired by the Miao people’s lifestyle, tales and daily objects, we abstracted cutural elements representing their culture and had them merged in the design of Miáo. The final objects were hand crafted in Jingdezhen with the help of craftsman there.
