Visit to RCA London

Feb 2019

RCA London, as one of the most prestigious college in design and art, has attracted me a lot since my bechalors. I am very much interested in how art school especially RCA is doing design and their way of thinking because I once I received an offer to the program of Innovation Design Engineering (IDE) and I wonder if I made a right decision coming to TU Delft. It turned out to be either right or wrong, but just different ways of doing design. Students there complain a lot about school, but the design atmosphere there is much stronger than Delft and that is the only thinig I think I like better.
